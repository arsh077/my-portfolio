## Detailed Instructions:

### Step 1: Save Your Photo
- Right-click on your professional photo in the chat above
- Select "Save image as..." or "Save picture as..."
- Choose a location on your computer

### Step 2: Rename and Move
- Rename the saved file to exactly: `arshad-profile.jpg`
- Copy this file to your project's `public` folder
- Replace the existing placeholder file

### Step 3: Verify
- The file should be located at: `public/arshad-profile.jpg`
- Refresh your browser at http://localhost:5173/
- Your photo should now appear in both the hero section and about section

## File Requirements:
- **Filename**: Must be exactly `arshad-profile.jpg`
- **Location**: Must be in the `public` folder
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Recommended size**: 800x800 pixels or larger
- **Aspect ratio**: Square or portrait works best

## Alternative Method:
If you have the photo file on your computer:
1. Copy your photo file
2. Paste it into the `public` folder
3. Rename it to `arshad-profile.jpg`
4. Refresh the browser
